Frequently Asked Questions

  • Our Objectives?

    Encouraging our students to use their innovative thinking skills to approach and resolve market issues. Providing information to students about current happenings worldwide and in the field of marketing. To instill in children the value of cooperation and to help them discover their potential as leaders.

  • What is MarkSoc?

    At Marksoc, our primary objective is to acquaint students with the field of marketing, make them aware of its processes, and arouse their interest. Our main objective is to equip students with the abilities they need, including communication, interpersonal, platform, and persuasion skills. We want to mould them into mature, critical thinkers who can translate information into action.

  • How are we different?

    MarkSoc is Manipal University’s first marketing club.


    It’s a club based solely to increase the employability of the students and enhance their skill set. The members will be involved in organizing events that will habilitate them for management roles in the future. They will get a corporate-like experience as they will be marketing events, managing finances, and taking care of operations.


    Be a part of the most creative community of marketing enthusiasts and swim into the ocean of business and branding.

  • Why do you need us?

    For all its members, becoming a part of MarkSoc MUJ will be a unique experience. The club’s events will inspirit members to move outside of their comfort zones and engage with an immensely colossal group of individuals in a professional environment.


    Furthermore, students will be exposed to people in the corporate sector and will be able to build a useful network that will be salutary to their future endeavors.


    They will have the opportunity to meet and connect with inspiring men and women from all walks of life. This will inspire them to dream big, work hard, and inspire themselves.
